The Fördergesellschaft der Hochschule Konstanz e.V. has been supporting the HTWG Konstanz, its students and teachers since 1954. They support projects such as theses and study projects, institutions and initiatives that enrich campus life, or help those who find it difficult to study for financial reasons.
They approached me to redesign their entire corporate identity and create a new website. I rethought their identity from scratch, while maintaining a clear visual link to the HTWG itself. A new logo, modern typography and clear, concise messaging helped the Fördergesellschaft der Hochschule Konstanz e.V. bring their appearance into the modern age.
The website was built for performance and low cost: SvelteKit forms the basis of the front end, while the open-source CMS Strapi provides a user-friendly interface for adding and editing content. Everything runs smoothly on an inexpensive Linux VPS, helping the Fördergesellschaft reduce operating costs.

## Techstack
- SvelteKit
- Strapi CMS
- Resend
- Docker
- Postgres
- Traefik Reverse Proxy
